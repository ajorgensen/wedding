OUR STORY

In west of Boston, born of raised, on the soccer field is where they spent most their days...

Andrew and Jamie met in high school when they were 16 years old. Unable to drive together, as they both only had driving permits, the beginning of their relationship mostly consisted of driving behind each other and meeting at the movies. Dozens of Rib-B-Qs, school dances, and soccer games later, Andrew and Jamie graduated from Bromfield, with Jamie heading to Lafayette College and AJ on his way to the land of Huskies. They continued dating throughout college and law school, and now both work in Boston. In 2015, Andrew proposed on the Cliffs of Moher in Ireland, as Jamie’s family desperately tried to hide in the field behind them. Jamie and Andrew will be getting married after 11 years of dating (longer than most Hollywood marriages. RIP Brangelina), and would be so honored if you could join them! Sláinte!

CEREMONY AND RECEPTION

The ceremony and the reception will take place at the Barn at Gibbet Hill in Groton, Massachusetts. The barn is over 100 years old, and is located next to the Gibbet Hill Grill, which is one of the only restaurants in New England with a produce farm on site. The bulk of the produce for Gibbet Hill is grown at the farm and kept at its own root cellar and winter greenhouses.
